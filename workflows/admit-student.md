# Admit a student

Admit an applicant and convert them into an enrolled student in UniFlow.

---

## About this task

The admissions workflow ensures that:
- Applications are reviewed.  
- Offers are issued.  
- Accepted students are recorded as official students.  

Only users with the **Admissions Officer** role can admit students.

---

## Before you begin

Make sure:
- The applicant has submitted an application.  
- All required documents are uploaded.  
- The application status is **Under Review**.  

---

## Procedure

1. Log in to UniFlow.
2. Go to **Admissions** → **Applications**.
3. Search for the applicant.
4. Click the applicant’s name to open their profile.
5. Verify the information. 
If information is missing, request it before proceeding.
6. Click **Actions** → **Issue Offer**.
7. Select:
   - Program  
   - Term  
   - Tuition plan
8. Click **Send Offer**.
Result: UniFlow emails the offer letter to the applicant.
9. If the applicant accepts:
    - Open the applicant record.
    - Click **Convert to Student**.
  
### Result

UniFlow:
- Creates a student profile.  
- Assigns a student ID.  
- Links the student to the selected program and term.  

---

## What happens next

Once admitted, the student can:
- Enroll in courses.  
- Receive a tuition invoice.  
- Appear in academic and financial reports.
